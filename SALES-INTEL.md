## What a $97/mo Client Looks Like

A $97/mo Jobs Done client is a local trade service business with these characteristics:
- **1-10 employees** (owner-operator or small crew)
- **Established but invisible** -- good word-of-mouth, zero or weak online presence
- **No website, or a bad one** -- built on Wix/Squarespace years ago, not mobile-optimized, no contact form
- **Inconsistent reviews** -- maybe 5-20 Google reviews, no system for getting more
- **Missing calls** -- no after-hours coverage, no automated follow-up
- **Chasing invoices** -- no online payment link, still doing paper or Venmo
- **No time for marketing** -- too busy doing the job to sell the next one

These businesses are not failing. They are busy and profitable at small scale. The pitch is: "You're leaving money on the table every week. We fix that for $97/mo."

## The Pattern

Every business above shares the same profile:
- **Good at the work** (proven by reviews)
- **Bad at being found** (no website, no digital presence)
- **High-ticket services** (HVAC, plumbing, roofing = $3K-50K per job)
- **Price-sensitive but ROI-positive** (our $97/mo is recovered in minutes of a single job)

The sales conversation is not "buy our software." It is: "You are losing money every day. Here is the specific number. We stop the bleeding for $97."
