## [FINDING] What Makes AI Small Business Posts Go Viral

Studied top performers from last 30 days. Pattern is clear:

1. **Show results, not features** — "We booked 200 jobs this month automatically" beats any feature list
2. **Speed narrative** — "Set up in 48 hours" is a hook. Use it.
3. **Proof of concept demo** — Video of the actual thing working beats any copy
4. **Piggybacking trending conversations** — One dev posted a demo as a REPLY to a viral AI post and got $10K contract in 24 hours
5. **The tamale shop model** — Family business, relatable, AI made it easy, went viral with 22M views — authenticity + AI = massive reach
