## TOP COMPLAINTS BY COMPETITOR

### Broadly
- **Overpriced for what you get** — $399/mo before setup fees is steep for small contractors who only need review management
- **Limited integrations** — doesn't play well with field service software (ServiceTitan, Jobber, etc.)
- **AI features feel thin** — "AI receptionist" complaints about missed leads and poor call handling
- **Long onboarding** — $350 setup fee + slow ramp = frustration before they even see results
- **Support slow to respond** — small team, long ticket queues

### Podium
- **Nickel-and-dime pricing** — Core starts at $399 but phone numbers, 10DLC fees, and extra SMS credits stack fast
- **Complicated contract terms** — annual commitments with painful cancellation
- **SMS credit limits frustrate high-volume users** — 250 credits/mo on Core runs out fast for active businesses
- **Too enterprise-focused** — small contractors feel like second-class customers
- **Support quality inconsistent** — lots of "transferred to another rep" complaints

### Birdeye
- **Price shock at renewal** — starts at $299 but real costs climb fast with AI add-ons
- **Complex onboarding** — steep learning curve for non-tech contractors
- **Feature bloat** — overwhelming dashboard for users who just want reviews and messaging
- **Multi-location pricing punishing** — costs balloon quickly for businesses with 2+ locations
- **BBB complaints** — unauthorized charges, billing disputes documented on ComplaintsBoard

### Thryv
- **Predatory billing** — 96 BBB billing complaints; unauthorized charges; double billing; charges continue after cancellation
- **Impossible to cancel** — 6-month mandatory auto-renewing contracts; customers report needing to cancel their credit cards to stop charges
- **Holds your assets hostage** — website domains, Google Business listings, and social accounts controlled by Thryv; difficult to retrieve on exit
- **Marketing promises vs. reality** — sales reps overpromise; actual SEO/lead results consistently disappoint
- **Not HIPAA compliant** — disqualifier for any health-adjacent contractor
- **Trustpilot: 2.4/5** — massive gap between curated G2 (4.3) and real-world reviews
- **356 BBB complaints in 3 years**, 124 closed in last 12 months alone

### Wix
- **Billing trap** — auto-renewals spike 30–195%; renewal notifications buried in marketing emails
- **14-day refund policy enforced like a weapon** — even when Wix makes the error
- **No human support** — only 29% of users reach a live rep; rest get AI bots
- **SEO is weak** — 3.4s load time vs. WordPress 1.2s; limited meta tag control; no custom XML sitemaps
- **Template lock-in** — switching templates means rebuilding from scratch
- **Not built for contractors** — general website builder with no field service, scheduling, or review management features
- **PissedConsumer: 1.5/5** (376 reviews); Sitejabber: 1.3/5
